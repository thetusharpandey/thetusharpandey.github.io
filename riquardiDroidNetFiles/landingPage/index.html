Introduction to Riquardi

The stage of last-mile delivery to the hands of the customer of any item is a challenging task due to the randomness and inconsistency events (objects/hurdles/people/animals etc) often in a unique order for every case present. Making it so difficult to automate or even assist by technology to a concrete extent that we still use humans to execute the task. The presence of humans in this stage implies things like Confusion in figuring out the destination - implying extra time to delivery, Slow speed of the executive, and also the risk of human to human contact having far from ideal outcomes possible, as we saw in CoVid-19 transmission, to the cases of deliveryAgent-customer argument/scuffles.

Riquardi intends to use Drones called 'Droid' to automate and streamline the delivery process to an extent that shortens delivery time by a considerable margin, reduces customer to employee contact to ZERO, and increased the visibility of the brand while in transit to multiple times more than a human with a T-Shirt

Intended Structure

I wish to make it a startup, Launched to serve people some legal, edible products via Droids flying from the dispatch location straight to the location of the customer, This implies a consent between the source and destination of the Droid to have a clear intent of a flight. Riquardi agrees to not fly over No-Fly zones, provided the constraints are explicitly listed ensuring NO to Just-Harmless ambiguity.

We take location data from the user just like humans do, We take the path above ground level, passing around/over-from-a-considerable-height some private property just like humans do, and Don't stand at your door while you arrange money...thereby being present inside your property, looking inside. Payments are always online.
