Introduction to PunctureBuddy

The state of automobile sector in India is not bad at the moment. All the big brands coming in, some huge aquisitions, great Exits and interesting entries!! But have you even put some energy and time into thinking what makes these cars running on the roads, who allows them to be there for eternity... untill some legal filter decides that from then onwards, so and so vehicle be taken off the road?

PunctureBuddy intends to serve that strata of soceity.. our Mechanics called 'Pilots', (for they will take us flying high to laurels) to Organise and streamline the mechanic service delivery process to an extent that shortens service time by a considerable margin, reduces customer hassle to ZERO, and increases the visibility of the brand while in transit to multiple times more than a shed with a board that though barely legible... says "Mechanic"

Intended Structure

We wish to make it big, but just "Starting up", Launched to serve people some basic road assistance in shortest time from the Mechanic dispatch location straight to the location of the customer. We wish to have a clear intent of a Service. PunctureBuddy agrees to not obstruct traffic or other transit while in the process of providing assistance to people

We take location data from the user just like humans do, We take the path towards you that makes you feel safe and supported in a spot where you know nobody... except your PunctureBuddy who's coming to help you. Payments are always online.
